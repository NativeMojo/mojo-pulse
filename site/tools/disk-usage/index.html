Disk usage

See where the disk went.

“Disk almost full” is a fact. Where it went is the useful part. Pulse draws your storage as a map — every rectangle a folder, sized by what it's hoarding — and lets you walk straight to the problem.

Live capture from the app.

A map of your storage

One glance separates the landlords from the tenants: the 80 GB Library, the caches nobody remembers creating, the downloads folder quietly eating a quarter of the drive. The largest-items list rides alongside for the greatest hits.

Drill in, zero out

Click any rectangle to descend into it, keep clicking until you're staring at the actual culprit, then Reveal in Finder and deal with it. Scan your home folder or point it anywhere else.

Fast, and polite about it

The scan runs in parallel and cancels instantly. It's session-only by design — no persistent index, no filesystem watchers grinding away after you close the window. Your Mac stays fast; that's the whole point of Pulse.
