IP lookup

Ask who an IP address really is.

An address in your logs, a connection you didn't expect, or just “what does the internet see when I show up?” — paste any IP and get the verdict, the geography, and the network behind it.

Real screenshot from the current release — addresses blurred. Click to zoom.

The verdict up top

Before the details: is this address clean? Pulse leads with a risk score and calls out anonymization — VPN exits, Tor, proxies, datacenter ranges — and known-bad reputation, so you know instantly whether to care.

Routing and ownership

The network panel names the ASN, the owning organization, the ISP, and the connection type — the difference between “someone's home broadband” and “a rented datacenter box” is usually the whole story.

Including your own

One click on “Use my IP” shows your public address the way every site you visit sees it — which is also the fastest way to confirm your VPN is actually doing its job.

Under the hoodUser-initiated, single-address lookups against our geo service, cached locally. Independent of the connection map's opt-in — using this tool sends only the address you typed.
